## The setup

Enterprises don’t fear MCP—they fear **unowned tools** with **data exfil** superpowers.

## Mental model

Governance is **continuous**, not a checkbox before launch.

## Walkthrough

- Risk-tier servers: dev vs prod vs regulated data.
- Mandatory reviewers for tool schema changes.
- Automated checks: secret patterns, network egress, filesystem scope.
