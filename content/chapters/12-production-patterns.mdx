## The setup

Production is **policy + observability + rollback**, not “we deployed a binary.”

## Mental model

Your MCP layer is an **API gateway** with extra philosophical problems.

## Walkthrough

- Centralize auth; propagate identity on tool calls.
- Emit structured audit logs for mutating tools.
- Version tools; deprecate with dual-registration windows when needed.

Models amplify mistakes. Production patterns exist to **slow mistakes down**.
