## The setup

MCP doesn’t remove attackers—it **organizes** where they show up: descriptions, tool outputs, third-party servers, and over-broad tools.

## Mental model

Four primitives to remember: **description injection**, **rug-pull updates**, **cross-tool laundering**, **schema confusion**.

## Walkthrough

### Mitigations that survive contact with reality

- Tier servers: read-only vs mutating vs break-glass.
- Pin versions; review diffs on upgrades like any dependency.
- Separate “planner context” from “executor context” where feasible.

The model is not your user—it’s an **adaptive interface** between users and your systems.
